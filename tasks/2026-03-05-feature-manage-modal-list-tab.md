# Replace delete/import buttons with a "Manage" modal on the list tab

On the list tab, remove the two separate buttons (delete and import) next to the search bar. Replace them with a single "Manage" button (with a gear icon) that opens a modal. The modal should provide actions for bulk delete, import, export, and any other management operations.
