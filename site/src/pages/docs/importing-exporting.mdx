# Importing & Exporting

Amber supports importing cards from other spaced repetition apps and exporting your collection.

## Importing

### From Anki

Export your Anki deck as a `.apkg` file, then upload it through the Amber web app's upload view.

### From Mochi

Export your Mochi deck as a `.mochi` file and upload it the same way.

### Format support

The importer handles:

- Plain text front/back cards
- Cards with code snippets
- Basic formatting

Tags from your source deck are preserved where possible.

## Exporting

You can export your entire card collection from the web app. This is useful for backups or migrating to another tool.

> No lock-in. Your cards are always yours to take with you.
