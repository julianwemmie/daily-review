# Notifications

Daily Review sends email reminders when you haven't reviewed in a while. These are designed to be helpful, not annoying.

## Escalation schedule

Reminders follow an escalating pattern based on how long you've been inactive:

- **1 day** — gentle nudge
- **3 days** — you have cards piling up
- **7 days** — your retention is dropping
- **14 days** — final reminder

After 14 days, emails stop. We won't keep bothering you.

## Managing notifications

### In the app

Click your profile icon and toggle **Email notifications** on or off.

### In email

Every notification includes a one-click **unsubscribe** link at the bottom. No login required.

## Email provider

Notifications are sent via [Resend](https://resend.com). They come from a `noreply@` address associated with Daily Review.
