# Reviewing Cards

Open the Daily Review web app to review your due cards. The review flow is straightforward but powerful.

## The review flow

1. A card's **front** (question) is shown
2. Type your answer in free-form text
3. The LLM grader evaluates your response against the card's **back** (answer)
4. You see your **accuracy score** and feedback
5. Select your self-rating: **Again**, **Hard**, **Good**, or **Easy**
6. FSRS schedules the next review based on your rating

## Free-form answers

Unlike traditional flashcard apps, you don't need to match an exact answer. Write your response in your own words — the AI grader understands paraphrasing, partial answers, and different levels of detail.

The accuracy score (0-100%) is informational. It helps you gauge how well you knew the material, but **you** choose the FSRS rating.

## Grading modes

You can toggle between two modes in the review UI:

- **AI grading** — the LLM evaluates your answer and provides a score with feedback
- **Self grading** — you reveal the answer and rate yourself without LLM evaluation

Switch between modes at any time during a review session.

## Triage

New cards enter a triage queue before joining your review rotation. In triage, you can:

- Accept the card as-is
- Edit the front or back
- Delete cards you don't want
